Minami Odori means "South Avenue" in Japanese. This road runs east-west
from [Nishi Odori](/wiki/Nishi_Odori "wikilink") to [Higashi
Odori](/wiki/Higashi_Odori "wikilink"), passing [NIMS](NIMS "wikilink") and
[Kojima Denki](/wiki/Kojima_Denki "wikilink").
